About Tranium

Tranium (real name John) is an American gaming and variety YouTuber known for his Funny Moments and VRChat videos, amassing a community of over 1.23 million subscribers worldwide.

He created his channel on April 7, 2012, but didn't upload his first video until September of that year, quickly rising to fame with comedic gameplay highlights.

JOHN is based in Austin, Texas, and frequently collaborates with peers like Crispy Concords(🤤), Soup(is love, soup is life) to make content.
